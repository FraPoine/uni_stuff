# 🎤 **Presentation: What Makes a Great Leader?**

---

## 🧠 **1. Leadership Begins with Self**

> “Be a leader for yourself before being a leader for others.” – Lars Sudmann

### 🔍 Self-Awareness

- Know your strengths and blind spots.
    
- Ask yourself: _“Who was the worst leader I ever had, and why?”_
    
- Learn from what _not_ to do.
    

### 🪞 Self-Reflection

- Take 2–5 minutes each morning:
    
    - _Who am I today?_
        
    - _Who do I want to be?_
        

### 🔄 Self-Regulation

- Reframe challenges: _How important is this?_
    
- Choose your reactions with intention.
    

### 🎯 Self-Perception

- Aim to see yourself as others see you.
    
- Feedback is essential for growth.
    

---

## 💡 **2. Core Traits of Great Leaders**

### 💪 Courage

- Comes from one thing: someone who believes in you.
    
- Great leaders take risks _on people_ and _for people_.
    

### 🧭 Integrity

- Do the right thing—even when no one’s watching.
    

### 🗣️ Communication

- It’s not just about talking, it’s about listening.
    
- Speak in a way that invites others to listen.
    

### ❤️ Empathy

- Care for the people who do the work.
    
- Make others feel safe to be themselves.
    
- Treat your team like you’d treat your kids—with trust and care.
    

---

## 🔭 **3. Vision & Passion**

> “Nobody remembers a string of words. They remember a vision.”

### 🚀 Vision

- A leader offers direction, not just decisions.
    
- It's not about control—it's about clarity.
    

### 🔥 Passion

- Real leadership comes with enthusiasm.
    
- It’s not just mindset—it’s a _strategy_.
    
- If you want to inspire, be inspired.
    

---

## 🌐 **4. Navigating Complexity**

> “What got you here won’t get you there.” – Roselinde Torres

### ❓ 3 Questions Every Leader Should Ask:

1. **Where are you looking to anticipate change?**
    
    - How do you spend your time?
        
    - Are you preparing for what’s next?
        
2. **What is the diversity of your network?**
    
    - Are you connected with people different from you?
        
    - Diversity brings new patterns and new solutions.
        
3. **Are you courageous enough to abandon the past?**
    
    - Step out of your comfort zone.
        
    - Defend new ideas, not old habits.
        

---

## 🧬 **5. Leadership Is a Practice**

> “Leadership is not about being in charge. It’s about taking care of those in your charge.” – Simon Sinek

- Great leaders are students of leadership:
    
    - They read, they listen, they _stay curious_.
        
- Leadership is not a title—it’s a responsibility.
    
- It requires **great personal sacrifice**.
    
- It’s about **building others up**, not being the hero.
    

---

## 🛠️ **6. Rethinking the Role of the Leader**

> “Leadership is embedding the capacity for greatness into people and practices—not into yourself.” – David Marquet

- Leadership is **decentralized**.
    
- It’s not about charisma—it’s about creating systems and cultures that work _without you_.
    

---

## ✨ Final Message:

Being a leader doesn’t mean having all the answers.  
It means having the courage to listen, the humility to grow, and the passion to serve.

> Start by leading yourself—because that’s where all great leadership begins.
