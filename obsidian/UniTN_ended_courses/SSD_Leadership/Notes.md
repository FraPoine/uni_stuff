	- epic crush management show off more than perfect preparation

		- interesting ones have an advantage on the ones how are boring

	- **who speak more, regardless of what they say**
	- **appear confident, regardless of competence**
	- **are perpetually busy, regardless of what they're doing**

## What Makes a Great Leader?
- Leadership is less about getting people to follow u to the future and more about getting them to co-create it with u
- 3 functions that has to fulfill
	- Architect
		- innovating work is about collaboration between different people with different needs
		- collective genius
	- Bridger
		- Forge partnership outside your organization
		- u need to have a bridge to what u need (talents and tool)
	- Catalyst
		- Accelerate co-creation across the entire ecosystem
		- u need other organization to be able to innovate
- These roles require new ways to thinking about power
