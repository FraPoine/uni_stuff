Data
- dependency on training data
	- ML models require high-quality, up-to-date and diverse training data to be effective
	- this applies to both benign and malicious training data
- Data privacy
	- ML operations might raise privacy concern, especially in cases where network data contains sensitive information
ML model design and training issues 
- false positives and negatives:
	- ML models can generate false positives or false negatives. achieving the right balance between these two is challenging and often requires tuning the model parameters and thresholds 
- resource intensive 
	- training and maintaining ML models can be computationally intensive and resource-demanding 
- Over-fitting and generalization:
	-  ML models can overfit the training data, meaning they perform well on the training data but poorly unseen data
